Chicken Burrito

Home

Description

Chicken burrito recipe is perfect for meal prepping. They are easy to assemble, cook, and freeze away.

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 3 cups spanish rice
  • 8 large flour tortillas

Instructions

  1. Break rotisserie chicken into bite sized pieces.
  2. Heat pan to medium heat and put beans on the pan until they are warmed. About 5 minutes.
  3. Assemble burrito by laying a tortilla flat on a clean space. Place cheese, chicken, beans, and rice in a line along the tortilla. Fold the sides of the tortilla which are at the ends of the line and roll from the other sides.
  4. Heat burrito by placing on medium hot pan. Heat each side until browned and crispy. About 2 minutes each side.
  5. EAT!
